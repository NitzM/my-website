Nitzan Micher

My research focuses on consciousness, semantic processing, and unconscious cognitive processes, emphasizing the clarification of methodological issues in distinguishing conscious from unconscious processing. I conduct empirical research using rigorous statistical modeling and data analysis.

Research & Projects

  • Conscious Perception and Semantic Processing: Investigating the role of conscious awareness in semantic information processing.
  • Methodological Clarity in Consciousness Research: Addressing methodological issues to accurately measure conscious and unconscious cognitive processes.
